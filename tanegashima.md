**The Tanegashima Space Center** (**種子島宇宙センター**, **Tanegashima Uchū Sentā**) (**TNSC**) is the largest rocket-launch complex in Japan with a total area of about 9.7 km². It is located on the southeast coast of Tanegashima, an island approximately 40 km south of Kyushu. It was established in 1969 when the National Space Development Agency of Japan (NASDA) was formed, and is now run by [JAXA](zz_jaxa.md).
