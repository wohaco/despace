> <small>*Terms:* **Wallops** — English term with no analogues in Russian. **Уоллопс** — Russian equivalent.</small>

Wallops Flight Facility is a rocket launch site on Wallops Island on the Eastern Shore of Virginia, United States, just east of the Delmarva Peninsula and approximately 160 km north-northeast of Norfolk. The facility is operated by the Goddard Space Flight Center in Greenbelt, Maryland, and primarily serves to support science and exploration missions for NASA and other Federal agencies.

It consists of three separate land plots with a total area of 25 km². It was founded in 1945, the first successful launch was made on February 16, 1961.
