**Landing gear (LAG)** ‚Äî [structural](sc.md) element of the [spacecraft](sc.md), designed to ensure the landing of a spacecraft on the surface of a planet or other celestial body. Variants of name (execution):

   - crumpled (or squash) landing gear (LAGS)
   - inflatable (or pneumatic) landing gear (LAGP)
   - parachute
   - [rotor](iu.md)
   - spring (pneumatic, hydraulic) landing device
   - torus landing gear (LAGT)
