> <small>**Jiuquan** — EN term. **Цзюцюа́нь** — literal RU translation.</small>

**Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center** (JSLC; *Chinese: 酒泉卫星发射中心*) is a Chinese space vehicle launch facility ([spaceport](spaceport.md)).

It is the largest spaceport in China (until 1984 — the only one) and the only one used in the national manned program. It also launches military missiles. The polygon at the cosmodrome has an area of 2 800 ㎞².
