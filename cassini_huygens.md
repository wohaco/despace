## Spacecraft

The mission consisted of two main elements: the ASI/NASA Cassini Orbiter; and the ESA-developed Huygens Lander.

The combined Orbiter and Lander is the third-largest unmanned interplanetary spacecraft ever successfully launched, behind the Phobos 1 and 2 Mars spacecraft, as well as being among the most complex.
